The GERMAN CHEMICALS LIMITED (GCL) sees itself as a global service provider for the whole textile industry. 100 GCL employees work at developing innovative products to contribute to our customers' success, many of whom belong to different industries. Even the smallest details are developed together by an exchange of experience to be able to combine and reflect the requirements of a wide variety of cultures. This enables us to convey our strict rules and regulations as part of a personally created value chain, even in which people take liberties with environmental protection and sustainability.

Having started a venture 20 years ago, today we are privileged to be a founding member of one of the most competitive chemical company with emphasis on efficiency in operations, reliability for customers and trust on sustainable development. For us, sustainability means aligning economic success with environmental and social responsibility. In the last few years the German Chemicals Limited faced a series of prolonged crises. These crises allowed us to gain invaluable experience and guided us to newer avenues. We would like to thank our employees, customers, suppliers and other stakeholders, for their dedication and support. We are committed to improving the efficiencies in our operations and differentiating ourselves in the market-place through customer focused innovation in product and services, so as to build a stronger and sustainable future for our Company and our associates.

Our range of product starts from pretreatment to finishing dying. As well as printing and washing. We have also some special product for dyeing and finishing. GCL offers an excellent service maintaining standard of perfection in all areas of its operations. It also maintains smaller quantities of stock to meet needs of different customers. Dynamic sales and highly qualified foreign trained technical staff are on call to provide the best possible solutions. It has diverse product range for knit, woven and denim garments made from any kind of fibers. GCL has been and is committed to meet its standard. Clients can rest assured of total confidentiality in every step of the process. From the inception of the order to the delivery of final product, maximum control is exercised at all stages. Nothing is left to chance. All wastages are destroyed in the presence of senior officials. Physical security is given top priority. The factory is under round the clock surveillance.
